How it works

Three steps, one minute.

  1. 01

    Add your keys

    Paste a provider key. It's encrypted with AES-256-GCM on the server before it's written — the database only ever holds ciphertext.

  2. 02

    Pick a service

    Choose a provider and a model and start a chat. Model routing, RAG, and custom MCPs are next on the roadmap — same vault, more services.

  3. 03

    It runs on your key

    We decrypt in memory, call your provider with your key, and return the response. Their pricing, their limits, their bill — straight to you.

Key custody

Your keys go in.
Only ciphertext comes out.

A leaked key spends your money, so custody is the whole job. Here's exactly what happens to one.

🔒

Encrypted at rest

AES-256-GCM. Each row is just iv, tag, and ct — authenticated ciphertext. The plaintext key is never stored.

⛓️

Hardened sessions

Passwords are scrypt-hashed. Sessions are HMAC-signed, HttpOnly, Secure cookies — no tokens sitting in your browser storage.

🛡️

Private transport

Served only over HTTPS, through a dedicated Cloudflare tunnel isolated from everything else on the box. No public ports opened.

↩︎

No markup, full control

We never see your provider invoice and never charge per token. Add a key, or revoke it, in one click whenever you want.
